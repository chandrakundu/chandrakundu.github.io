About

Hello, I’m Chandra Kundu, a passionate Ph.D. candidate in Big Data Analytics and a dedicated researcher at the University of Central Florida’s Department of Statistics and Data Science. My research delves into the development and application of advanced machine learning and optimization techniques, particularly focusing on deep learning-augmented non-convex algorithms for large-scale matrix completion and Euclidean Distance Geometry problems. Supported by prestigious NSF grants, my work addresses critical challenges in areas such as image and video processing, hyperspectral imaging, biomedical data analysis, and sensor networks. My research contributions are aimed at pushing the boundaries of data science, uncovering meaningful insights, and solving complex real-world problems through innovative data-driven solutions.

In addition to my research endeavors, I am deeply committed to education and mentorship. As an instructor of record at UCF, I have successfully guided over 300 undergraduate students through courses like Principles of Statistics, fostering a robust understanding of statistical methodologies and data science principles. My role extends beyond teaching, as I mentor students in advanced data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization techniques, preparing them to excel in the dynamic field of data science.

My technical expertise spans a wide array of tools and technologies, including Python, R, MATLAB, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and cloud platforms like AWS and GCP. This diverse skill set enables me to craft sophisticated solutions and interactive visualizations that translate complex data into actionable insights.

Recognized for my contributions, I have received numerous awards, including the Algorithms for Threat Detection (ATD) Challenge Winner by the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency and multiple scholarships and fellowships from UCF. These accolades reflect my commitment to excellence and my drive to make impactful contributions to the field of data science.

Through the synergy of rigorous research and effective teaching, I strive to advance the frontier of data science and artificial intelligence and contribute to solving pressing societal challenges with data-driven innovations.
