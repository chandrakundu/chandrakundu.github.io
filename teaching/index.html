Teaching

My teaching philosophy centers on the 3Cs: Curiosity, Clarity, and Connection. I believe effective instruction should move beyond content delivery to spark intellectual exploration and empower students to become confident, competent contributors to their fields.

I foster Curiosity by integrating real-world data and applications from diverse fields (healthcare, business, social sciences) to demonstrate the practical relevance of statistics and data science. I promote Clarity by structuring every class with clear learning goals, using guided notes to facilitate active learning, and providing prompt, actionable feedback through low-stakes quizzes and mid-semester surveys. Finally, I build Connection by establishing a welcoming classroom culture where students feel safe to ask questions, learn collaboratively through guided notes, and know that I am genuinely invested in their academic success and growth.
