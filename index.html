Chandra Shakhar Kundu

Ph.D. candidate specializing in Big Data Analytics at the University of Central Florida

News & Updates

  • March 2025: Presented at the 59th Annual Conference on Information Sciences and Systems (CISS 2025) in Baltimore, Maryland.
  • February 2025: Presented a poster at the Annual ASA Florida Chapter Meeting 2025 in Orlando, Florida.
  • October 2024: Presented a poster at the SIAM Conference on Mathematics of Data Science (MDS24) in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • May 2024: Presented a poster at the SIAM Conference on Imaging Science (IS24) in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • October 2023: Received the Dean’s Fellowship, a one-time award recognizing my academic achievements and commitment to excellence in undergraduate teaching.
  • September 2023: Won the Algorithms for Threat Detection (ATD) Challenge issued by the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency (NGA). Our work contributes to solving critical challenges in predicting the trajectory of geopolitical events.

  • Deep Learning-Augmented Optimization for Inverse Problems
  • Anomaly Detection in High-Dimensional Data
  • Mathematical Foundations of Deep Learning and Machine Learning Models
  • Robust and Scalable Algorithms for Big Data Analytics
